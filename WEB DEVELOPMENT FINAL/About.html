Lets get to Know Me

About me: Introduction and Overview

Alright, strap in for the tale of Theodore Wolfe, born and bred under the Florida sun but now making waves along the rugged seacoast of New Hampshire. Picture me dribbling hoops on the court one minute, then spinning lyrical magic with my poetry the next. Music? Oh, it's in my veins, especially those classic tunes that take you back in time. And as for aesthetics, well, let's just say I'm a connoisseur of all things vintage, always seeking out that timeless vibe that sets my soul on fire.

What I do: Personal and Educational/Professional Background

Step into my world, where I'm not just a photographer; I'm a time traveler wielding a camera as my portal. Across continents, I journey, seeking out the pulse of life and freezing its heartbeat in pixels. Whether it's the quiet intimacy of a portrait, the adrenaline rush of sports, the sacred vows of weddings, or the untamed spirit of wildlife, I'm there, capturing every heartbeat, every flutter of life's wings. Through my lens, I don't just capture moments; I create history, crafting visual epics that echo through eternity.
